<h1>**Foodie**</h1>
**Foodie - The Game, is a simple game where you live every human's dream, 
You play as a mouth and there's food falling down from the sky.**
**<h1>Keybinds**</h1>
> A -> Move Left<br>
> D -> Move Right<br>
> Esc/P -> Pause<br>
> Esc -> Back<br>
